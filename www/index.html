Where is my transaction in the mempool?

The chart displays a mempool snapshot from my node. The mempool contains unconfirmed transactions. Transactions paying a higher feerate (fee per size) are usually included earlier than low feerate transactions.

Transactions are grouped by their feerate. The size of each group corresponds to the amount of transactions in the group. Groups with the highest feerate are at the top of the chart. Lower feerate groups appear at the bottom. The horizontal lines show a rough estimate of which transactions a greedy miner would include in the next block.
